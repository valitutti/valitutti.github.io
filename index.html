Welcome!

I am Alessandro Valitutti, a physics teacher at the Liceo Scientifico Galileo Galilei in Trento. For many years, I have been passionately teaching, developing ideas and educational tools to help my students better understand physics and its applications. At the same time, I have pursued a research career focused on computational creativity, computational humor, and affective computing. On this site, I will share useful materials both for educational purposes and for those interested in my research and studies. Thank you for visiting and enjoy exploring!
